tinyRSA, Keep It Simple

TinyRSA is a simplified implementation of the RSA cryptosystem for learning purposes.
Choose a keylength between 2 and 1024 to generate a key and start encrypting ! (keep in mind the length of the key is double the size of your input ex: 1024->RSA2048)
